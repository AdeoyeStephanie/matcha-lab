
## Matcha Lab
**currently in progress**

I had my first Matcha in March 2024 and i've loved the drink since then. 

On the average, i make a good matcha latte as a sweet treat once or twice a week and over time my aesthetic photos and recipes have dissapeared in my gallery along with multiple images of my calc notes.

The inspiration behind matcha lab was to have a place to track all my recipes (not in my notes app) and be able to tweak them with AI



## Tech Stack
Front-end: react.js + next.js + react

DB: Supabase (PostgreSQL)

AI + API: Gemini 

Deployment: Vercel?
